About Lana del Rey

Elizabeth Woolridge Grant was born on June 21, 1985 in New York, New York. She first started performing under the name of Lizzy Grant. She would edit her own music videos on YouTube and found her fame when “Video Games” went viral in 2011.

Fun Facts: She is a cancer.

Albums:

Born to Die

Born to Die was released in 2012. It reached No.2 on the Billboard 200 and sold more than 7 million copies around the world. It was certified platinum by the RIAA.

Ultraviolence

Ultraviolence was released in 2014. It debuted at No.1 on the Billboard 200 and is certified platinum.

Honeymoon

Honeymoon was released in 2015. Lana has commented that this is a tribute to Los Angeles. Once again, it reached No.2 on the Billboard 200 in the US and it reached No.1 in Ireland and Australia.

Lust for Life

Lust for Life was released in 2017 and it is a more lively album compared to others in the past. )
